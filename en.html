What do parents and Olympians think

“Even when I studied in the U.S at Harvard, I still find that their educational philosophy is no different from Olympia’s.”

Student-centred learning is one of my favourite philosophies. And I'm proud to know that this has always been one of the key features of The Olympia School since its establishment.

Nguyen Vu Thanh An

2013 Olympia alumni, graduated with a Master’s degree from Harvard University, USA

“I’m proud to have my daughter study at The Olympia Schools.”

Applying to Universities/Colleges with the counseling from UCC without external consultancy helped my daughter to be more active and independent. Until now, he has already grasped the school’s study schedules and daily routine. I can sense the confidence and certainty in him. Thank you, Olympia.

Mrs. Nguyen Phuong Ha

Mother of Thao Anh - 2019 Olympia alumni
