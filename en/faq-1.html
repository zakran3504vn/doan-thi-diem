School programs and activities

Do Olympia students have to take extra classes?

The learning program at Olympia will aid students to grasp knowledge right in its classes. Students need to invest extensive time for preparation prior to learning new lessons and complete research after class on their own. 

The school will have plans for effective learning guidance and for examinations within each stage. Underperforming students will also receive support from teachers. Extra classes and test preparation are based on the family's needs.

Do students have to do homework? What is the volume of homework?

Education at Olympia does not put pressure on homework but rather focuses on the values of duties students need to accomplish at home: train them with the awareness and capability for self-study and enhance their ability to prepare for new lessons by themselves.

Can Olympia students enter universities in Vietnam?

For students with early orientation (from grade 9), studying elective classes and being coherent with such orientation, they often will achieve good results for their high school exams, which is the foundation for enrolment in suitable universities in Vietnam. Results depend on students' efforts.

If students are no longer able to attend Olympia, are they guaranteed to still be able to attend good public schools?

This depends on each student's abilities and the requirements of the individual schools. Initially, when transitioning to a new learning environment, students may face challenges as they adjust, since all changes require time to adapt. However, with education and knowledge guaranteed based on the Ministry of Education and Training's programs, as well as the self-directed learning, research abilities, and problem-solving skills taught at Olympia, students will quickly overcome these hurdles.

In cases where families change their plans for their child's education, if they inform the teacher early, they will receive additional guidance on the educational pathway from the subject teacher and, if necessary, from the school psychologist.

During the learning process, what experiential activities does the school offer?

Experiential learning is one of the school's main teaching methods, included in it are the experiential activities within each subject according to grade levels, held in different school years.

Moreover, the experiential activities are diverse and rich in content and format. With interdisciplinary projects and experiential activities, each grade level has at least one project that combines classroom learning with outdoor activities, field trips, and heritage studies. For learning projects, students use interdisciplinary knowledge to solve real-world problems, produce reports, and earn grades for the subjects involved in the project.

 

The experiential programs, part of the LiFE program or other extended learning activities, are organized according to the school's specific characteristics. The experiential program includes: sports competitions, cultural performances, events such as Walkathon, Mid-Autumn Festival, Halloween, Teacher Appreciation, Christmas, Winter Festival, End-of-Year Celebration, Prom, "The Stage" music competition , "Olympia Film Festival" film competition, fundraising, sponsorship seeking, charitable activities, and community service.

How is the English proficiency of Olympia students measured at the end of each grade or educational level? Is it necessary to study English at language centers?

Olympia School is an examination center for international certifications organized by the University of Cambridge, UK, for students upon completion of the corresponding levels.

Besides, students can apply to participate in internationally standardized tests organized by the University of Cambridge (not compulsory), in particular:

  • Second graders who achieved Starters.
  • Third graders who achieved Movers.
  • Fourth Graders who achieved Flyers.
  • Fifth and sixth graders participate in the KET (Key English Test).
  • Seventh and eighth graders participate in the PET (Preliminary English Test).

Additionally, PTLC Olympia School is one of the official SAT test centers in Hanoi.

 

Students do not have to learn English in language centers if there is no further need for it.

How does the school's English environment help students develop their communication skills?

Students learn English everyday with native as well as Vietnamese teachers. The school has clubs facilitating students' learning and use of English like drama and debate clubs... Furthermore, the school also conduct English classes for staff and teachers of other subjects.
                               

What makes the school's English program especial?

The English program at Olympia Inter-level School is built upon the Cognitive Academic Learning Approach (CALP). This approach helps students, throughout their education from primary to high school, develop all four language skills (Listening, Speaking, Reading, Writing) in an integrated and comprehensive manner, rather than focusing solely on 1-2 language skills.

Additionally, students use foreign language skills as a tool to explore knowledge across various fields, develop foundational thinking skills, and foster lifelong learning abilities. The English program at PTLC Olympia is systematically and rigorously designed by the school's professional team in collaboration with leading educational experts both domestically and internationally. It is based on the Common European Framework for Reference (CEFR) and the 21st Century Skills Framework (Critical Thinking, Creativity, Communication, Collaboration). This ensures a commitment to language proficiency standards at all educational levels, not only equipping students well for any international standardized tests (Cambridge, IELTS, TOEFL, SAT) but also providing a strong foundation for their studies in any international educational environment in the future.

The teaching methods in the English program at Olympia are continuously researched and innovative, incorporating the most advanced and effective teaching methods globally, including Inquiry-Based Learning, Deep Learning, and Project-Based Learning. The integration and balance of these advanced teaching methods with a curriculum developed in alignment with the school’s educational orientation successfully fosters a love for learning and motivation among students, while also helping them develop comprehensive knowledge and thinking skills, alongside language proficiency.

 

 

How many English lessons do students have per week?

According to each grade, the number of English lessons will vary from 5 to 7 lessons/week (55 minutes/lesson). Other than English periods in the subject program, Olympia students also have English practice, learning Theater Arts in English and several science subjects taught in English.

After graduating from Olympia Inter-level School, what certificate or diploma will students receive?

After graduating from Olympia Inter-level School, students will receive a certificate of their completion of the academic program (Diploma of Graduation) at Olympia.

For students opting for American electives , apart from a certificate of academic program completion at Olympia, they will receive the U.S. High School Diploma of our partner school - the Marianapolis Preparatory School, Connecticut, U.S.A.

University consulting, study abroad and foreign exchange

Does Olympia Inter-level School have career orientation and university counseling activities for students?

Olympia is the first school in Vietnam to have a University Career Counseling (UCC) office in its vicinity. The counseling officers of the UCC office have had years of experience, studying and working both in Vietnam and abroad. Even more unique is that the UCC office is designed to provide Olympia students exclusive counseling, assisting them finding the most suitable orientation for themselves and their future.

Why do students require counseling about universities, overseas study and career orientation?

University counseling at Olympia is a process of selecting and enrolling in universities, in which students participate in every phase. The first phase is where students explore their own needs, aspirations and personal capability. The next important ones include guiding and helping students achieve their targets after graduation.

Văn phòng UCC đem lại những lợi ích gì cho học sinh và gia đình của học sinh?

  • Văn phòng UCC hướng dẫn cụ thể, chi tiết học sinh trong việc tự tìm hiểu và ra quyết định liên quan tới con đường học tập, lựa chọn nghề nghiệp và cuộc sống tại nước ngoài.
  • Văn phòng UCC hỗ trợ học sinh khi nộp đơn và xin học bổng cũng như trong thời gian du học nước ngoài.
  • Văn phòng UCC đảm bảo thư giới thiệu được đầy đủ và chính xác.
  • Học sinh sẽ được nhận sự bảo vệ về mặt quyền lợi trong khi nộp đơn từ các cố vấn của văn phòng UCC.
  • Học sinh được nhận tư vấn chất lượng từ các chuyên viên tư vấn chuyên nghiệp, là thành viên của các tổ chức tư vấn chính thống, danh tiếng cả trong và ngoài nước.

What can the UCC office guarantee for students and their families?

The UCC office guarantees that students will receive unbiased, honest and quality advice so that they can select universities that suit their needs, aspirations and personal capacity. However, the UCC office does not fully guarantee students' admission to universities, scholarships and Visa grants for study abroad. In reality, there is no universal answer for every scenario.

How can Olympia students have access to more information from universities?

Every year, the UCC organizes various educational showcase and study abroad programs at the school, participated by hundreds of universities in the world. Through these platforms, students can find out more about universities and hear from university counselors. Apart from that, workshops and group discussions with unviersity representatives are also held by the UCC office throughout the school year.

When should my child begin their university counseling process?

Students should carry out the process as soon as possible since the selection of universities relies heavily on self-discovery and self-understanding. If cannot prepare in a sooner time, students should start from the beginning of grade 9.

How does the UCC office charge for their services?

The UCC office is one of numerous offices providing free services that Olympia specifically reserves for its students. Students will submit any fees related to the process of building their study abroad profiles directly (if any) and Visa grant fees to universities, embassies or relevant organizations.

What can my child do get in top universities?

First and foremost, students need to understand the requirements and demands of these universities. Secondly, students need to assess their own strengths and weaknesses since such complete reflection will help them identify whether their goals should be those institutes. Thirdly, students are required to map out an action plan and finally, carry it out. This plan needs to be monitored closely, regularly and carefully. Throughout this process, students will receive direct assistance from the UCC office.

Can UCC help my child get Visa grants?

The UCC office will provide the best assistance for students in achieving Visa grants. However, whether the student is granted Visas or not depends on embassies and there is no one-size-fits-all answer for every scenario. Despite this, the UCC takes pride in the percentage of Olympia students getting their Visas to this day.

Do I need to participate in my child's university counseling process?

Because the process of university counseling does not only affect the student directly, but also impacts their family, the UCC encourages parents to actively take part in it and book sessions to visit the UCC office if necessary.

If my child uses the UCC's services, should we consult other study abroad counseling centers?

The decision is completely up to parents. However, with many years of operational experience, Olympia's UCC office believes in its understanding of students and provide more extensive assistance compared to private counseling offices. That being said, if you feel more comfortable receiving a wider range of opinions, the school encourages you to do so.

Would UCC provide its service if my child studies in Vietnam?

The UCC office assists every Olympia student regardless of their location of study. In addition, the UCC office coordinates with students' teachers to help them understand more about themselves and to achieve their aspirations of enrolling in their desired universities.

Do Olympia students have opportunities to meet with foreign students in Vietnam?

Every year, Olympia Inter-level School welcomes student cohorts from partner schools such as Miss Porter's, Marianapolis (U.S.A.) for cultural exchange and study. At the same time, Olympia students are also incentivized to learn abroad in the U.S., helping them get familiar with the American educational system and find out more about their future studies.

Facilities and services

What is the scale of the school? What is special about the school's facilities?

Designed by Perkins Eastman Architectural Firm (U.S.A.), the campus at Olympia Inter-level School has a modern style with facilities of international standards, built on a 10,000 square meter plot of land. With over 50 spacious classrooms and 2 science laboratories, teachers and students have access to internationally standardized classroom equipment. Furthermore, subjects like music, arts, and information technology also have their own functional rooms, fully provided with facilities and supplies.

Olympia fully equips facilities and prepares specialized rooms of international standards for students. The basketball court of Olympia Inter-level School is a focal point, designed according to the National Basketball Association's (NBA) standards. Apart from that, students have access to functional sports facilities like swimming pool, fitness room, martial arts room, dance room. Bringing the Olympia theater into operation creates a space for theater performances, conferences, and international and domestic events, giving opportunities chances to have diverse experiences and develop their passion and hobbies.

 

How does the school's bus service operate?

Olympia Inter-level School's bus service covers the entirety of Hanoi with 50 routes, extending to suburban districts like Ecopark, Binh Da, Vinhomes Long Bien, An Duong Vuong, Splendora, Co Nhue, Gamuda, Gia Lam. The average pick up and drop off time varies from 6:30 to 17:30. Olympia Inter-level School selects quality car service from reputable establishments in Hanoi, many of which have operated continuously for 12 years. 

At Olympia Inter-level School, training activities for supervisors and drivers occur twice a year to ensure service quality. The car model used by Olympia Inter-level School is the 16-seater Ford Transit manufactured from 2016 onward.

How does the School handle the events of their cars experience traffic congestion or delays?

The school is always in a state of readiness and activeness to avoid delays. Each car has its own supervisor onboard to guarantee safety and be on call with parents, timely notifying them of changes regarding early or late departures and arrivals.

Do students have their own blankets and bed sheets in the midday break?

The school provides blankets and bed sheets for students in the midday break.

How are student meals managed?

Students have breakfast, lunch, and snacks at the school in a self-selection format. The dining area is spacious, equipped with a food warming system and has age-appropriate tables and chairs. The school schedules two meal shifts according to grade levels to avoid overcrowding in the dining room. The school regularly inspects the sources of food to ensure food safety and hygiene.

All the food used in the school's meals is sourced with clear traceability, and 90% of the vegetables are organic. The kitchen serves nearly 200 dishes each week, including traditional Vietnamese food, European cuisine, plant-based options (vegetarian dishes), and various snacks.

 

What are the school's medical activities like?

The medical room at Olympia Inter-level School is responsible for managing, monitoring, and caring for the health of students, teachers, and staff across the entire school. Having been in operation for nearly 10 years, the medical department at Olympia Inter-level School consists of two medical rooms staffed by three doctors and medical officers, exceeding the professional qualification standards required for school healthcare.

The medical activities at Olympia School are carried out according to standardized procedures set for scholl healthcare by the Ministry of Health in collaboration with the Ministry of Education and Training. Key activities implemented by the school's medical department include health care for students, teachers, and staff; organizing annual health check-ups for students and staff; daily monitoring and care for underweight and overweight students; creating tailored meal plans for special cases; and completing insurance paperwork for students and staff.

 

School psychology

How do the school psychologists provide support?

School psychologists will accompany teachers, siblings and students to find solutions to their own problems.

In addition to the differential screening and general mental health screening for students at the whole school, when coming to the school psychology room, teachers, brothers and sisters and students can completely choose the images. formula like:

  • Individual counseling/therapy for struggling students;
  • Student group counseling/therapy (small or large);
  • Organizing discussions and seminars for parents on child and adolescent psychology and parenting skills;
  • Mentoring and coordinating with families, teachers, staff, and school administration in finding solutions to students' mental health needs and needs;
  • Provide and share useful information and resources for teachers and parents.

 

 

 

As student, how will they be helped?

You will have a place to share all of the issues listed above. At the school psychology room, your child will have a private meeting, where he can express and get answers about his concerns about body development, emotional changes and actions. Children will be consulted to solve problems in friendships, heterosexual friendships, relationships with parents and teachers.

As parents, how can we get help with problems with our children?

Parents play a huge role in being the fulcrum or orientation for their children's healthy development. Still, when it comes to children's maturation, especially through puberty and adolescence, it's understandable for parents to have difficulties. When parents encounter such problems, school psychologists are available to listen and advise parents with scientific information about their child's development as well as positive parenting skills such as how to talk. , resolve conflicts with your child or set goals...

Every profession has principles, so what is the working principle of School Psychology at Olympia?

We operate in accordance to these principles:

Principle #1: Listen and respect

  • Listen and respect: teachers, parents and students are able to share whatever please them, we do not judge right or wrong and fully accept and respect people's opinions.

Principle #2: Information security

The School Psychology office always values each person's privacy and so we guarantee every problem raised by teachers, parents and students remain confidential. In detail:

  • In the event of students arrive at the counseling room by themself, every issue raised by the student will be kept confidential.
  • We only reveal student's information to those responsible (their parents, advisors...) in the following three scenarios: 1) The student is attempting to cause self-harm or harm to others; 2) The student agrees to share information with relevant individuals to receive better assistance; 3) The student reports receiving threats.
  • In the event of the student being directed to the counseling room by their parents and/or teachers, we will communicate with representatives and relevant individuals about general information regarding professional help to better aid the student.

Principle #3: Provide solutions

  • Arriving at the School Psychology office at Olympia, teachers, parents and students are not only listened to and understood, but also provided with new updates to communicate and figure out solutions for their current situations.

Principle #4: Operate according to procedures

At the School Psychology office, all services will be carried out in accordance with the five enclosed steps procedure, ensuring logic and security. The general steps are as follow:

  • Direct interview of identifiers of the problem (parents, students, teachers)
  • Meet with the individual in need of counseling and open a consultation/treatment file.
  • After 2-3 meetings with the individual in need, school psychology specialists plan a consultation strategy (number of sessions, frequency, necessary assistance) and communicate with parents and the homeroom teacher. 
  • Assess every 3 sessions and maintain close contact with parents and teachers (homeroom and subject) if the individual in need is a student.
  • End shift, assess shift and plan out upcoming monitoring steps.

Child Protection Policy

The Olympia Schools' Child Protection Policy

The Olympia Schools (Olympia, “the school”) is committed to the safety of our community and students including protecting students from harm (to self and from others), and we are proud to be a school where child protection and child safety are our top priority. We take very seriously our duty and responsibility to protect and safeguard the interests of all children. We also recognize that effective child protection work requires procedures, cooperation, and a workforce that is competent and confident in responding to child protection situations. As part of The Olympia Schools’ commitment to keeping all students safe, the Child Protection Policy aims to:

(1) raise awareness of child abuse and child protection

(2) identify risks and cases of possible abuse of children

(3) provide guidance on child protection and safety.

We review and revise (if necessary) the policy annually, or after an incident pertaining to child protection issues when responding from an interested party, or when significant changes to the school’s operations are introduced.

Please further review the Child Protection Policy, including its principles, rationale, and guidelines, to ensure proper recognition, response, and reporting of concerns. These policies and procedures reinforce The Olympia Schools' dedication to safeguarding children and outlining the reporting process.

General questions about the school

What year was Olympia inter-level school founded?

Olympia Inter-level School is a pioneering educational system, developed based on Dream House educational system which was founded in 2003. 2023 marks the 20-year formation and development of Dream House - Olympia educational system.

What grades does Olympia provide education for?

The educational system at Olympia operates in four grades:

  •      1. Kindergarten (4-5 years old)
  •      2. Primary School (grade 1 - grade 4)
  •      3. Middle School (grade 5 - grade 8)
  •      4. High School (grade 9 - grade 12)

Especially, apart from the Bilingual Program, Olympia High School also deploy the Integrated Program so that parents and students have a wider range of choices for their educational orientation at University level. All four levels operate at Trung Van new urban area, Nam Tu Liem, Hanoi (the only location).

Is Olympia an international school?

Being an inter-level school in Vietnam, Olympia's educational program guarantees goals in accordance to Vietnam's national educational curriculum, boasting a continuity in connection between grade levels and especially invested in maintaining the cultural identity of Vietnam amongst the young generation.

The specialized target of the educational program at Olympia is preparing students with the ability to integrate themselves in their education in international environments, as well as to succeed in future endeavours with the mindset of endless learning. The program approximates those advanced in top-ranking nations for education.
