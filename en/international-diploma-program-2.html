International diploma program

  • Overview
  • Our Programs
  • Who we are
  • News & Events

The International Dual Degree Program is an integrated education program between the Marianapolis Preparatory School's educational program, USA and the Vietnam High School Education Program. Upon completion of the program, students receive a high school diploma from the Vietnamese Ministry of Education and Training and an American high school diploma from Marianapolis School.

With the orientation of personalizing learning to suit learners' abilities and needs, the school offers elective subjects including:

(1) Electives subjects are career-oriented and in-depth study (Startup, Business, Marketing Communications, Psychology, Scientific Research...).

(2) International certificates (IELTS, SAT, AP, American, IB...).

(3) Clubs for developing talents and social activities (Sports, Fine Arts, Music, Radio, Volunteer work...).

The Integrated Program at Olympia supports studying abroad and transferring credits at international universities, according to the orientation and wishes of families and students.

Our Programs

Our main curriculum

Vietnam – USA international program at Olympia school is the application of the superior 3:1 learning model: 3 years in Vietnam and 1 year in the USA. In the first 3 years in Vietnam, students will study both Vietnam’s high school program with Olympia’s teachers and USA’s high school program online with Marianapolis’s teachers.

See details
Experiential learning program

Experiential learning program is a system of experiential learning courses, academic exchange and cultural exchange, in which extracurricular activities, internship and college orientation are organized throughout the school year. This program is designed to enable students to get in touch with reputable schools, businesses, organizations from Vietnam and abroad such as: Miss Porter’s – one of the leading girl’s boarding schools in America, Fulbright University Vietnam, Forbes, VinTaTa, Facebook, Google, etc.

See details
Methodology

The International Diploma Program at the Olympia Schools is based upon Blended Learning methodology, which is highly applicable in many advanced education systems in the world. With this method, students can have optimal development with the subjects from Vietnam’s general education system, as well as conquering subjects from American general education system, from which they are equipped with global citizen’s knowledge and skills to prepare for the future experience in international academic environment and tackle challenges at work and in their lives.
