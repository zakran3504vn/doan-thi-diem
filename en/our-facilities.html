Our facilities

The 10,000 m2 campus was designed by the American architecture company Perkins Eastman, creating a space for learning, playing, thinking, and creative activities for a wide variety of students. Olympia believes that every corridor hall, and classroom can be a place to learn. Everything constructed aims toward students with friendliness and familiarity, ensuring that Olympia is a place where our students can learn and share knowledge every day.

Osaka yard

The Osaka yard was named after a red flower planted in Olympia from the beginning of the school. This sunshine yard was students’ favorite place to play, walk or outdoor learning activities.

Olympus library

Science garden

This is where species are grown, seeds are sowed, and where students can learn about various plant species in science lessons LiFE… This is a favorite space for inspiring outdoor lessons for Olympia teachers and students.

Classroom

Olympia has 50 classrooms which are fully equipped: light, air-conditioners, projectors, speaker, felt/mica board. Outside the classroom is the personal locker of the students.

Olympia education system

Olympia education system include Olympia High School, Olympia Middle school, Olympia Primary school, situated in the Trung Van new urban area, Nam Tu Liem, Ha Noi

Dining hall

Olympia has 3 dining halls: 2 dining halls in the Mind building and the other in the Heart building. Three of them can accommodate 750 people. The cooking process is safe and hygienic. The Olympia kitchen has been a long-lasting impression on many students' generations and parents.
