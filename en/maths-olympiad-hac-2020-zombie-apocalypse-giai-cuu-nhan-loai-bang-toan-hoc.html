Maths Olympiad HAC 2020: “Zombie Apocalypse - The path to saving humanity” with Mathematics

Have you ever wondered how maths can be the key to saving humanity in case of a Zombie apocalypse? On November 28, 45 teams coming from 11 international, private, and public middle schools in Hanoi will be participating in a contest of mathematical skills named Maths Olympiad with the theme of a “Zombie Apocalypse - the path to saving humanity”. This is an English Maths competition that is hosted annually for middle school students, opened to all international schools across Hanoi.

Maths Olympiad was founded by HAC - Hanoi Activities Conference, whose members are International Schools of Hanoi. Ambitious to discover a new perspective on the mundane subject of mathematics for students through games and activities that allow participants to use their mathematical skills to solve real life challenges. The competition often attracts a lot of students and is held alternately at international schools, for example: Concordia, BVIS, BIS… This is a playground of exploration for students to discover hidden interest and to socialize with fellow participants.

Ever since the first year hosting this event, The Olympia Schools have already had a team participating as special guests, and from 2020, The Olympia Schools is officially the hostess for this event. From content building to execution will be carried out by Olympia’s mathematics department

The theme of “Zombie Apocalypse - The path to saving humanity” came to us after the event of COVID-19. Students are required to find solutions to save humanity from the dangerous pandemic. Having to overcome 4 main rounds of obstacles and finding answers to math questions in order to protect our planet.

Students that are participating in this upcoming competition have already overcome the challenges of the previous school round. The event will be divided into 2 sectors for Juniors and Seniors
